Video Duration for YouTube™

General

  • Enable extension
  • Always show on the top corner of the video
    • Which corner to place the controller
    • Show remaining time and time difference (temperarily) when watching in non-1x speed
      Example: "10:00" means you can finish the video on current speed in 10 minutes)
    • Show potential time saving or addition (if any) to watch the remaining video in the current non-1x speed
      Example: "▼2.00" means you can save 2 minutes watching the remaining video on current speed
    • Show watch progress (e.g. 30%)
  • Enable keyboard shortcuts

Speed

Shortcuts QWERTY keyboard. For other layouts, ignore letters and use the same key positions
